Hamburger recipe

Description

In this recipe we will make very juicy and delicious hamburgers.

You will need a grill or a grill-pan

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Hamburger patties
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo or any other sauce you like

Steps

  1. Heat the grill or the grill-pan until hot
  2. Cut the buns in 2 and place them on the grill for a few minutes
  3. Put the hamburger patties on the grill and season with salt and pepper
  4. After 8 mins flip on one side and after 8 more mins remove them from the grill
  5. Add lettuce on the bottom of the hamburger
  6. Add the hamburger itself
  7. Add slice of cheddar cheese on top (-- optional --)
  8. Add tomatoes
  9. Add onions
  10. Add ketchup and mayo or any other sauce
  11. ENJOY!
