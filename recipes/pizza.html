Pizza recipe

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make a catlicious looking pizza with just a few ingredients you should have in your kitchen.

(No cats were hurt or used in this recipe)

Ingredients

  • Pizza dought (-- if you don't have pizza dought already (witch you should, after all you are making a pizza, right?) visit this website --)
  • Tomato sauce (-- or any other sauce other than this --)
  • Mozzarella (-- or any type of cheese you like, but again keep in mind you are making a pizza not macaroni and cheese. --)
  • Salami
  • Black olives (-- or green ones with red pepper inside --)
  • Other toppings of your choosing (-- I used this on my pizza and it turned DELICIOUS --)

Steps

  1. Flatten the pizza dought until it takes the form a cat's head
  2. Add your sauce on top of it and gently smoothen it out on all the surface of the pizza
  3. Add the salami and olives (-- and the other toppings of your choosing --)
  4. Try to make it look like a cat (-- optional step --)
  5. Preheat the oven at around 180 degrees celsius
  6. Leave the pizza in for 20-30 mins depending on what kind of color you want your cat pizza to be
  7. Cut in 6 or 12 slices depending on how much you can eat
